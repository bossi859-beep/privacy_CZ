1Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how ARENIX.app processes personal data when the platform is used.

ARENIX.app is built on a partner-based model. The platform primarily serves Partners who create their own Arenas, invite members of their community and verify community activity in practice.

Our goal is to process only the data needed for platform operation, security, partner access management, Arena functionality and protection of system integrity.

↑ Contents

2Basic Data Model of the Platform

ARENIX.app works with two basic groups of users:

  • Partner – a person, brand, community, mentor, educator or community leader who creates an Arena and invites members of their community.
  • Arena Participant – a community member who enters an Arena through an invitation or entry link from a Partner.

The platform is not designed as a public database of traders. ARENIX does not automatically take over a Partner's community as its own commercial database.

↑ Contents

3Data We May Process About Partners

For Partners, we may process in particular:

  • name or brand name;
  • contact details;
  • community name;
  • community size;
  • a link to Telegram, Discord, a social network or another community channel;
  • data from the partner application;
  • data about created Arenas;
  • technical and operational data related to use of the platform;
  • communication related to partner access.

We use this data mainly for:

  • creating and managing partner access;
  • enabling Arena creation;
  • assessing the suitability of a community for further cooperation;
  • ensuring platform security;
  • protection against misuse;
  • operational communication with the Partner;
  • development of the partner relationship.

4Data We May Process About Arena Participants

For Arena Participants, we aim to process only the minimum scope of data needed for entry and operation within an Arena.

This may include in particular:

  • email address;
  • user identifier or nickname;
  • information about entry through a specific invitation;
  • assignment to a specific Arena;
  • activity in the Arena;
  • leaderboard position;
  • Arena Credits;
  • technical data needed for security and platform operation.

ARENIX does not need to know the real identity of an Arena Participant unless this is necessary for a specific function, security reason, legal obligation or special partner terms.

5Partner Community

The Partner builds their own relationship with their community.

ARENIX provides the tool, interface and infrastructure. ARENIX does not automatically sell, take over or use the Partner's community as its own commercial database.

If a Partner invites members into an Arena, the data of those participants is used primarily for the operation of that specific Arena, display of results, access management and platform security.

The Partner is responsible for inviting their members in a lawful and fair manner.

6Arena Credits, Leaderboard and Activity

The platform may process data about Arena activity, ranking, score, Arena Credits and other data needed to display results according to the rules of a specific Arena.

This data is used for Arena operation, leaderboard display, evaluation of community activity and protection of platform integrity.

Arena Credits have no monetary value and are not intended for payout, exchange or use as a substitute for money.

16Contact and Platform Access

ARENIX.app is built on a partner-based model.

Parties interested in partner access may start through the official ARENIX.app landing page.

Arena Participants do not enter the platform through direct public registration. Participant entry is possible through an invitation or entry link from the Partner who organizes the relevant Arena.

If the user is a member of a specific community, they must request Arena access directly from the Partner who organizes that Arena.
